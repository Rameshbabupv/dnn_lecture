🎓 First Classroom: 128 Students!

tf.keras.layers.Dense(128, activation='relu', input_shape=(784,))

👥 What's Happening?

128 smart students each look at ALL 784 pixels
Each student specializes in finding different patterns!

Student 1 says:

"I see curved lines!"

Student 2 says:

"I see straight lines!"

Student 3 says:

"I see corners!"

Each of the 128 neurons connects to ALL 784 input pixels.
That's 128 × 784 = 100,352 connections! 🤯

🚪 ReLU is like a bouncer:

✅ Positive numbers: "Come on in!"
❌ Negative numbers: "Sorry, no entry!"
🎯 Result: Only confident, positive predictions move forward!

This helps our network focus on strong, confident signals and ignore weak, uncertain ones!
